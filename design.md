# Design System Strategy: Lumina Teal
 
## Core Vision
This design system is built on a foundation of clarity, approachability, and modern fluidity. By combining a professional teal palette with maximal corner rounding, we create a user interface that feels both trustworthy and exceptionally friendly.
 
## Visual Language
 
### Color Palette
The system utilizes a "Fidelity" color approach, ensuring that our primary brand colors remain vibrant and consistent across all surfaces.
- **Primary Color**: #38A191 (A balanced, energetic teal used for key actions and brand presence).
- **Secondary Color**: #B2DFDB (A soft aqua used for tonal support and background accents).
- **Mode**: Light. The interface emphasizes cleanliness and readability through a bright, airy aesthetic.
 
### Typography
Our typographic hierarchy balances personality with utility.
- **Headlines**: Plus Jakarta Sans. Chosen for its modern, geometric flair and high-energy personality.
- **Body & Labels**: Inter. A workhorse typeface that ensures maximum legibility across all screen sizes and densities.
 
### Shape and Structure
- **Roundedness**: Level 3 (Maximum). All components—including buttons, cards, and input fields—utilize pill-shaped or highly organic curves. This removes visual tension and creates a "soft-touch" digital experience.
- **Spacing**: Level 2 (Normal). The layout follows standard breathing room guidelines, balancing information density with clear visual hierarchy.
 
## Component Principles
- **Buttons**: Fully rounded (pill-style) with the Primary Teal background to drive interaction.
- **Surfaces**: Light, clean containers with soft shadows and generous corner radii.
- **Interaction States**: Transitions should be smooth, mirroring the fluid nature of the rounded visual language.
